The voltage timeseries shows on the X-axis the timestamps and on the Y-axis the average voltages.

The current timeseries shows on the X-axis the timestamps and on the Y-axis the average currents.

Voltage probability density function

The voltage probability density function shows on the X-axis the voltages and on the Y-axis the probability of occurrence.

The current probability density function shows on the X-axis the currents and on the Y-axis the probability of occurrence.
